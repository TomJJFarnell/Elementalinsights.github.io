🏢 Myth: "Analytics is only for big corporations"

Reality: If you track sales, appointments, inventory, or even volunteer hours — you already have valuable data waiting to be analyzed. Size doesn't matter.

🗂️ Myth: "Our data is too messy to be useful"

Reality: Data cleaning is part of every project. In fact, messy data often reveals the most valuable insights once properly organized and understood.

🧮 Myth: "I'm not good with numbers or tech"

Reality: You don't need to be. I translate complex data into plain English recommendations you can act on immediately. No math degree required.
